Hi, I'm Ste-chan! I'm a traditional and digital artist based in Finland.

I've been drawing and writing stories ever since I was little. I started watercolor painting in 2020 during the COVID-19 pandemic as a way to relieve stress and improve my mental health. I still enjoy regularly using art as a way to express myself and explore the world. I'm inspired to create by things I enjoy, and I hope I inspire others to work on their own art as well!

Welcome to my website, I hope you enjoy your stay!
